The Warehouse

So, this is the warehouse in the Mission, near 16th St BART. It’s funky—homeless folks, trash, but also a ton of history. I’m in love with it. Here’s the plan: offices downstairs, a school on the second floor, and eventually, places for families to live above. Oh, and a rooftop with a statue of splitting arrows—kinda symbolic, you know?

The Farm

Then there’s the farm outside Seattle. We’ll go there for a few months each year to get our hands dirty and let the kids run wild. Nature’s the best teacher, right?

The Dream

I was homeschooled, and it shaped me in ways I’m still figuring out. I want that for my kids—freedom to learn, create, and mess up. Not in a fancy way, just real. This place is about that.
