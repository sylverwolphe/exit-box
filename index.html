Welcome to ExitBox.how

Hey there! I’m so glad you’re here.

This is a little corner for folks curious about unschooling—a place to explore learning that’s free, personal, and true to you. Whether you’re new to this or a seasoned wanderer, there’s something here for you: ideas, tools, and a small crew of kindred spirits.

About Unschooling

Unschooling is about trusting yourself—your curiosity, your pace, your passions. It’s learning what lights you up, when it feels right, and how it fits you best. I’ve been walking this path for a while, and I’d love to share what I’ve picked up along the way.

Here at ExitBox.how, it’s all about keeping things real and close-knit. We’re here to help you carve out your own way of learning, with a bit of support and a lot of heart.
